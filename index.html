Inflation Calculator

This inflation calculator helps you understand how inflation reduces the value of money over time. It shows what today’s amount will be worth in the future after considering inflation.

Inflation means the general increase in prices over time, which reduces the purchasing power of money.

Calculate Impact of Inflation










How Inflation is Calculated

Inflation impact is calculated using the following formula:

Future Value = Current Amount ÷ (1 + Inflation Rate / 100)Time

This calculator uses the same formula to estimate the future purchasing value of money.

Example of Inflation Calculation

If you have ₹1,00,000 today and the inflation rate is 6% per year for 10 years, the future value of that amount will be approximately ₹55,839.
